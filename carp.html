CARP Posters

REPETITION

For my repetition poster, I thought it would look cool if I used what I learnt in Adobe Illustator when doing the typography systems work, to make the words form around a guitar. This highlights the focus of the poster as it is to promote a film/ music festival.This also meets the aim of repetition as I used the lines formed around the guitar, repeating the title of 'True/False Film Fesitval' continously filling the page. This flows stabilty through the poster, allowing for the information within to be presented as confident and significant.

CONTRAST

To meet the aim of contrast I decided to use scale. I started with drawing the focus to the letters of T and F. Scaling the letters to the whole page you'd think would be the aspect making the poster seem important, however I think real draw in would be the minimal text within the poster. Just how the front face seems to scream urgency but when drawn in you aren't greeted with details and details, instead, the information you need. Things you always look for: what is it? where? when? I also created contrast in the colours of the T and F whilst also matching the theme within the festivals logo.
