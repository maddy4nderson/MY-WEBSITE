REPETITION

For this poster I thought it would look cool if I used what I learnt in Adobe Illustator when doing the typograpghy systems work, to make the words form around a guitar. This highlights the focus of the poster as it is to premote a film/ music festival.This also meets the aim of repetition as I used the lines formed around the guitar, repeating the title of 'True/False Film Fesitval' continously filling the page.
